## ğŸ“… Booking System

### How to Book an Appointment

#### **For Logged-In Users:**

1. **Navigate to Appointments**
   - Click **"Book Appointment"** on homepage
   - Or go to User Dashboard â†’ Appointments

2. **Fill Booking Form**
   - **Select Service**: Choose from dropdown (Haircut, Coloring, etc.)
   - **Select Stylist**: Pick your preferred stylist
   - **Choose Date**: Use date picker
   - **Choose Time**: Select available time slot
   - **Add Notes**: Optional special requests

3. **Submit Booking**
   - Click **"Book Now"**
   - Confirmation message appears
   - Appointment appears in your dashboard

4. **Manage Bookings**
   - View all appointments in your dashboard
   - **Cancel**: Click cancel button (before appointment time)
   - **Reschedule**: Edit appointment details

#### **For Walk-In Clients (via Receptionist):**

1. Receptionist logs in
2. Goes to "Create Appointment"
3. Selects client or creates new client record
4. Books appointment on behalf of client
5. Processes payment if immediate service
